Privacy Policy

MatchMentum - AI-Powered Resume Optimization

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Introduction

MatchMentum ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered resume optimization platform.

Important: By using MatchMentum, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Profile Information: Resume content, work experience, education, skills, contact information
  • Job Information: Job descriptions you analyze, companies you're targeting
  • Generated Content: Resumes and cover letters created using our service
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely by Stripe)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Analytics Data: How you interact with our service (if analytics enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 AI Processing

Your resume data is processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to:

  • Parse and extract information from uploaded documents
  • Analyze job descriptions and calculate match scores
  • Generate tailored resumes and cover letters
  • Provide ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization

AI Processing Note: Your data is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

2.2 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process subscription payments
  • Store your profile and generated documents
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related notifications

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Improve AI accuracy and performance
  • Develop new features
  • Monitor and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Supabase: User accounts, profiles, generated documents (encrypted)
  • Stripe: Payment information (we never see your full card details)
  • Your Browser: Temporary local storage for performance

3.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure authentication (email verification, OAuth)
  • Row-level security on database
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to data
Data Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email.
